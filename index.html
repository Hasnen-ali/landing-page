Who We Are

India's Largest & Oldest
Schools Exhibition

The Premier Schools Exhibition (PSE) is India's most prestigious platform connecting parents and students with India's top boarding and day schools. Now in its 21st year, PSE travels to 14 Indian cities and 10 countries annually.

Meet school principals face-to-face. Attend free education seminars. Find the right school for your child — all under one roof.

  • ✓ Free entry for all parents & students
  • ✓ Direct interaction with admission teams
  • ✓ Expert-led education seminars
  • ✓ Compare IB, Cambridge, CBSE & ICSE boards
